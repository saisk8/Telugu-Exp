Instructions

Please read them before you proceed.

General

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Use the latest Mozilla or Chrome browser to attempt this. This is not meant to be done on a mobile device.
  • The estimated time of completion is about 5-7 minutes.
  • Please complete the experiment in one go.

About the experiment

  • You will be shown a pair of Telugu alpabets for a duration of 2 seconds. after which they disappear.
  • You are required to rate the pair of Telugu alphabets shown in terms of their Dissimilarity.
  • Once you have rated the pair, click the next button, to get a new pair of alphabets
  • You will be presented with 30 such pairs.
    • which would constitute a set.
  • You will have to attempt 10 such sets.
